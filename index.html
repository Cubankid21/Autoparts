COMPANY PROFILE

Part of a Major Group

Sundaram Auto Components Limited is a part of the $8.5 billion TVS group.

Established in 1988

The company began operations in 1988 and has grown significantly since then.

Automotive Focus

It specializes in providing injection moulded plastic solutions to the automotive industry.

Growth Philosophy

Their core philosophy is "being the first choice for all stakeholders."

Extensive Experience

With over 25 years of expertise, they have a long history of developing plastic parts and solutions for automobiles.

Multiple Locations

Operations are spread across six locations: Hosur, Chennai, Nalagarh, Mysore, Bhiwadi, and Sanand.
